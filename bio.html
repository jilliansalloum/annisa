Get to know Annisa Whims

Annisa Whims is an acrylic, watercolor and oil based artist located in the Grand Rapids area. She is a self-taught surrealist artist with a foundation in realist works, who enjoys challenging the ordinary. Upon further understanding the relationship with herself and how that impacts the environment, her work has greatly flourished in recent years. Her degree in horticulture has opened her mind to the infinite symbiotic relationships in the natural world. Annisa has forged the understanding that change starts by looking inward; understanding the environment within ourselves first before our lives can become something more. Annisa aims to capture the curiosity, and perhaps even torment, that we face as humans on an emotional level in a physical world. For Annisa, painting is the medium between imaginary and the tangible.
